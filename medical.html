The Problem

Patients are researching you.
Something's making them leave.

Someone searches for a dentist or med spa, finds your practice, and then hesitates. Maybe the reviews aren't convincing. Maybe booking feels like too many steps. Maybe your website doesn't feel credible enough to trust with their health.

They close the tab and book somewhere else. You never knew they were there.

⭐

Not enough reviews

Patients trust other patients. If your review count or rating doesn't inspire confidence, they move on without calling.

🌐

Website credibility gaps

A slow, outdated, or unclear website signals risk to new patients. First impressions decide before they ever read a word.

📅

Booking friction

If patients can't book online easily, many won't bother calling. Every extra step loses a percentage of them.

📬

Missed inquiry follow-up

A new patient inquiry that doesn't get a fast response goes cold within hours. Most practices have no automated follow-up.

📍

Google profile gaps

An incomplete or unoptimized Google Business Profile ranks lower and sends fewer people to your site in the first place.

📣

Running ads before the funnel's ready

Paid ads to a broken booking flow burns budget. The funnel has to convert first — then advertising amplifies it.
